Great Place To Study has set a benchmark for institutes to meet global education standards.

Imagine your child feels safe and happy at school. Student Fulfilment Index enables you to play an active role in your child’s school life and create a positive learning environment. Student Fulfilment Index (SFI), a unique offering by Great Place To Study, gives all stakeholders, especially parents and teachers a platform to fine-tune existing education models. For the first time, students, parents, teachers and alumni will play an active part in designing the future of the education system. Through SFI, GPTS certifies institutions based on 7 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs). Schools that have met and exceeded the standards set by GPTS are recognised as a ‘great place to study’. Engineered by industry experts, SFI is designed to usher in a healthy and happy environment for children.
