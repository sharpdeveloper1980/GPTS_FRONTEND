OUR VALUES

Our focus is to create communication platforms between students and institutes, and to drive solutions for issues we care about.

Friendly Workplace

GPTS is structured like an open family that recognises the value of ideas and understands their potential to fuel innovative growth.

Happier Weekdays

At Great Place To Study, every day is “bring your passion to work” day. Our offices are filled with fun, frolic, and interesting conversations.

Choose Positivity

With a vehement passion for revolutionary education, the GPTS family is a multigenerational workforce that fully embraces and understands diversity.

Learn and Explore

The open culture at every level allows new members to have a safe space and an honest dialogue. We believe in a healthy exchange of ideas and knowledge.

Loving the new

We at GPTS believe that the future is made better when looked from different perspectives. We pride ourselves in swaying from tradition and striving for innovation that is charged with positive outcomes.

Constant learning

The GPTS family is a community of lifelong learners, responsible global citizens, and champions of our own success. Being an education firm, we preach less and learn more.
