Top Institutes are looking for you

 

Choose from the finest institutions in the country

With CDAP, enrolling in to your favourite institute has become easy and exciting. The Career Discovery and Acceleration Platform (CDAP) gives you the tools to navigate your search for a good college. It offers you the chance to find an institute that is a perfect fit for your interests and passion. With a click of a button, you will have doors of opportunity swing open just for you.

How can you reach out to them?
One
Complete your profile

Simply by filling out a form with your basic information, education details, interests and family information, you will be on your way to enrol in the programme. The information that you provide will be entered into CDAP and used to formulate a list of the best colleges for you.

Two
Upload your written & Video SOP

In your own words, talk about your interests through written and video Statement of Purpose (SOP) and reach out to the best universities. The SOP will boost your profile. The topics for the SOP will be provided by interested institutes with a stipulated time range.

Three
Shortlist your favourite colleges

You can make a list of your favourite colleges by simply clicking on the heart-shaped icon next to the college. If you want more details about the institution, a detailed university micro site will be made available to you during the process. Hence, allowing you to research thoroughly and narrow down your choices.

Four
Submit Required Information

Any institute that you add on your favourites list may ask you to submit some documents and information. Requirements may include marks sheets, identity proof, and transfer certificate among others. By submitting the necessary information, you will have the opportunity to get shortlisted by your favourite institute.

Five
Apply to your DREAM College

You can now directly apply to your dream college using our one-step common application form. By providing all the necessary information, you can apply to multiple colleges. The process of college admissions has never been this easy.

Six
Get notified on your application status

Once your form has been filled and submitted, you will receive updates on the status of your application form. It has an added advantage of tracking multiple applications in different universities. Get instant notifications on the progress of your application form on your personalised dashboard.
