Frequently Asked Questions

We are sure that you have many questions for us, and we are all about finding the right answers for you.

We have listed out many frequently asked questions that will help you better understand the process and navigate through the steps that will help you become a part of Great Place To Study. We recognise that coming on board with our organisation is just the beginning of a long-lasting relationship, and our team will guide you every step of the way.

How do I create my account on gpts.com?
Visit greatplacetostudy.com, click on the login tab and fill in the desired credentials to create your account on greatplacetostudy.com.
Where can I get my unique ID?
Your unique ID will be shared by school authorities to every student.
How can I get access to my dashboard?
After creating your account, click on registration and fill in your details to get complete access to your dashboard.
Is it necessary to fill in all the credentials in my profile section?
It is necessary to fill in all the required personal, family and education details in the ‘My profile section’ within your personalised dashboard.
Is my personal information protected?
Yes, we assure you that your information is safe with us.
How do I take the Career Discovery Test?
Click on the left menu bar on your dashboard and select the Career Discovery Test option to take the test.
When will I get my test results?
You will get your results within 48 hours of completing your test. We will send you an email notification as soon as the result are uploaded.
What is the significance of each work style?
Each of the 8 Work Styles is built as a combination of your score amongst 24 sub-factors (strengths) of the Thriving Index. Strengths relate to patterns of thought and behaviours that can be productively applied. When these are refined with practice and combined with skills and knowledge that you acquire, they give you a powerful means of moving your career forward.
Where can I find my Career Analysis Report?
You can find your report on your dashboard under the 'My report' section. We will also send you a copy of your Career Analysis Report via email.
How do I get access to career-related videos in the must watch section?
Take the Thriving Index Career Discovery Test to identify your inherit strengths. The robust platform will recommend a set of careers that are best suited for you. As per suggested career options, you will get access to highly informative career videos in the must watch section.
How do I bookmark my favourite videos?
Click on the ___ icon to add multiple videos to your 'My favourite' section.
How can I get access to all the career-related videos?
Post registration, you can get access to all career-related videos through the video career library. You can also view the entire video career library through the dashboard by clicking on the career library icon.
How do I get to know more about the career I wish to opt for?
You can directly search for different careers through the dashboard in the ‘Must watch’ section post registration. After you watch the career, you can click on ‘Know more’ to visit the dedicated career page.
FOR PARENTS
How can I make an account on gpts.com for my child?

Can we edit and upload video SOP again?
Yes, you can edit your video SOP after recording, but once uploaded, you will not be able to edit it.
How will CDAP benefit my child?
The Career Discovery and Acceleration Platform is designed to guide young minds choose a career that is compatible with their intrinsic abilities. Through psychometric assessment of their strengths, motivators and blind spots, students can set off on a path that is defined purely by their passion. The Career Library is the first-of-its-kind new-age visual platform that has been designed to inspire young minds to discover their passion and explore intriguing career paths. Engineered by Great Place To Study, the video library offers students expert guidance and dedicated support to navigate career streams and job profiles across all sectors.
What is the Career Discovery Test all about?
The Thriving Index Career Report offers insight into student suitability towards work styles, occupations and career profiles, enabling them to explore diverse career prospects at a very young age. Budding minds get access to inside information on a plethora of career paths from experienced industry experts to create a roadmap for a promising future. The programme offers students an opportunity to seek focused guidance from experienced counsellors, inspiring them to embark on a journey to realise their dreams.
What will be the duration of the Career Discovery Test?
The duration of the Career Discovery Test is approx. 20 minutes.
By when can I get a detailed career report for my child?
You will get a detailed and insightful career report within 48 hours of giving the test.
Are students restricted to the 5 best-fit career options as suggested by the report or they can explore more career paths?
Each student will get 5 career options as per his/her report but they can explore endless careers through our platform.
Can we give the Career Discovery Test more than once?
The student can undertake the Career Discovery Test only once through each account. He/she cannot give the career test more than once from the same account.
How can we get full access to career library?
Student can access the career library through his/her dashboard. In the ‘Much watch’ section, click on get complete access button to visit the main career library page.
How can we see videos for specific careers?
You will find the 'Must watch' section on your dashboard. Here, click on 'Know more' and you will get to see the career specific videos
How relevant is the content in the career library?
We have a dedicated content team who is continuously researching and updating the website and career content with information gained from trusted sources.

In which all platforms can we (the clients) promote ourselves before & past the event?
You can promote all the platforms across digital and mainline, and you would have to take care of the branding guidelines of the intellectual property. In case you don't have them please make sure you ask from the respective client servicing manager.
What is the process for certification?
Step 1: Filling the application form on the website. Post which a welcome mail is received. Step 2: Registration and data update on SFI Portal(Welcome mail will have details about the portal).Step 3: Receiving Satisfaction survey links and successfully conducting the survey in the institute. (Generating minimum 80% responses). Step 4: Receiving a detailed in-depth report on the collected data. Step 5: If the set certification criteria of 3 out of 5 rating are achieved, the institute is qualified to be called a “Great Place To Study”.
If an institution applies to get Great Place to Study Certification, will it certainly get certified?
Any institute achieving the 3 out of 5 rating is qualified for the certification. But there is no guarantee that all the institutes applying will get the certificate, depends on how the *stakeholders' rate it. *Stakeholders are participants in the survey. Students, their Parents and Teachers.
What happens in case an institution does not meet the certification criteria post the assessment?
In this case, the school is not qualified for the certificate. It will still receive the detailed report and branding material.
What happens when an institute meets the certification criteria post the assessment?
The institution will be informed and will be issued a certificate along with the brand guidelines. The certificate will be valid for a period of one year.
