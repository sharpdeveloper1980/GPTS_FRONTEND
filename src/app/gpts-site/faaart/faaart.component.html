The best possible way to draw the attention of a budding artist or an aspiring filmmaker
is to give them an opportunity to create and showcase their work.

Great Place to Study under their Great Challenge initiative have created a film making a contest, for Class 12 students who are passing out of school in 2020 and other young aspiring film students. The idea behind creating a filmmaking contest is to generate interest among students wishing to pursue filmmaking as a career option.

Objective our aim

The objective of #FAAART contest and the campaign is to reach out to the maximum number of students interested in the process of filmmaking.

FAAART Your Way Through! how to apply

Students need to apply online

5 socially relevant topics will be given from which the student will choose one.

Students will need to fill-up a form on a micro website dedicated to the #FAAART Contest & submit a 500 word script and a 2-minute film on the topic they choose.

Students will be able to upload all their information on the website.

A sample script will be provided on the website.

The entries will then be screened with the following weightage- Story: 60% | Direction: 20% | Picture Quality: 10% | Sound Quality: 10%

The Test of Talent process of evaluation

Top 100 students will be selected by the jury on the basis of script and direction.

These 100 students will then be invited to attend a 10-Day workshop.

During the workshop, these students will work on different assignments. The work completed during this workshop will further be evaluated and top 10 will be shortlisted.

There will be a gala celebration on the last day of the workshop where eminent personalities will be invited and the student films will be showcased in a screening.
