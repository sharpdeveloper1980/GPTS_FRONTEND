Career Discovery & Acceleration Platform

CDAP is a student engagement platform that facilitates career discovery and college search. It brings the world class cinematic content with verified information on various career fields and helps you to make the right career choice.

CDAP is one stop solution for the students right from the start of their decision making years till they end up in a college. It serves as a platform that gives students everything they need beginning from discovering their career's on the basis of their inherent strengths, counselling and video career library to inspire them to take the best decision on the basis of verified information.
