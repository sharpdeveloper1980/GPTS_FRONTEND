Dalham Foundation

Explore unprecedented opportunities in the non-STEM world and chart new career paths to success.

DALHAM is an independent, not-for-profit social enterprise dedicated to advance, promote and practice non-STEM education as a lifelong endeavour. The foundation is committed to helping students explore their passion and priorities as individuals and identifying their proclivity towards domains in non-STEM subjects.

Elements of DALHAM

Foundation for creative and liberal education

D-ART

DALHAM – Assessment and Review Tool

DALHAM – Assessment and Review Tool (D-ART) is India’s first comprehensive examination to enter the undergraduate world of creative and liberal education. The test evaluates 12 facets of a student, including emotional response, logical thinking, pattern of thoughts and reasoning. D-ART is now accepted by top colleges and universities in India for student enrolment.

Learn more

HFA

Human Factor Assessment

The Human Factor Assessment (HFA) is the first analysis tool that assesses the emotional and mental wellbeing of an individual. The assessment adopts the science of Thematic Apperception to offer insights into a student’s reaction to different stimulus and their perception of the world. The test also includes Application Rating, Video SOP and Social Skills Test.

Learn more

I-LEP

Integrated- Liberal Education Programme

I-LEP is DALHAM Enabled Curriculum for institutions, that promises to embed credible values in every individual to create a diverse mindset and produce many leaders in virtually every field of human endeavour. The idea is to create diversity in the mindset of every individual as a "diverse mindset" is the need of the hour in every space - engineering, medical, accountancy, policy making, leadership and so on
