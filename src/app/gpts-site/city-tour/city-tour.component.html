About Future readiness summit

In this growing age of technology and advancement, the world is gearing up for massive change and upgradation. It is not the same as it was 10 years back and hence we are sure that the future needs us to be prepared. To address the need of skilled professionals that will be created by the jobs of the future, Great Place to Study has started a special initiative. Here school counselors, principals and industry experts will be coming under one roof to discuss emerging career options and how to prepare the next generation for the challenge.

Objectives
& Outcomes

  • The summit will enable the school counselors to discuss the changing trends of the professional world and forthcoming job options in both STEM and non-STEM category.
  • It shall also introduce them to the upcoming and novel methodology of Psychometric Test that is based on positive psychology.
  • The summit will also enable the counselors to discuss the changing trend of the career options among the students and new careers in both STEM & Non-STEM.
  • Introduce them to upcoming and new methodology of psychometric test i.e. based on positive psychology.
