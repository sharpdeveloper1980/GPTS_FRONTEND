Benefits of career report

01
The report provides you feedback on your dominant Work Styles based on your dominant strengths as well as possible career pathways. Based on your strengths, the report helps map your career suitability across 24 Occupational Clusters and will then provide you the top 5 Occupational Clusters suitable for you.
02
Keep in mind that this report provides career recommendations based on your strengths profile only and it is not a measure of your ability, skills or aptitude. Your final decision on which career to pursue will need to consider other factors such as your skills, ability, macro job conditions, wages, availability of jobs, work conditions, educational qualifications etc.
03
While this report provides an overview of possible career options, it cannot include all possible jobs that exist (or will exist) across the globe. This is an exploratory tool for you to better understand and appreciate your strengths and orientations so you can prepare for the career(s) that you feel most passionate about.
04
Whether you are just starting out in your career, thinking about a career change, or considering higher education options, you will gain tremendous insights into your strengths and most suitable careers based on this report.
